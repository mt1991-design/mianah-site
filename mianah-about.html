Our story

Mama in Bloom

Mianah began with a single feeling — the quiet weight of a sleeping child, and how impossible it is to hold onto.

So we paint it instead

Every Mianah print celebrates the blooming, blush-cheeked wonder of motherhood in bold colour and thick, joyful brushstrokes.

Exaggerated rosy cheeks, oversized florals, whimsical proportions and a fairytale glow — art warm enough to grow up beside.

Made for mothers, everywhere

Every piece is made to order and shipped, with love, to families across the world — from Lagos to Madrid to Kyoto.

Because the tender years pass in a blink, and some moments deserve a place on the wall.
